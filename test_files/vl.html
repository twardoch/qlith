Organize Fills To Layers and Apply Masks

Layers Made Simple

Think of layers as transparent sheets of paper—each one can hold its own fills, effects, and masks without cluttering the rest of your design. For instance, you can place your sketch lines on one layer, add a wave fill on another, and keep a halftone pattern on a third. Need to rearrange something? Just drag that layer above or below the others. Vexy Lines’ layer-based workflow keeps even the most complex projects organized, letting you hide, lock, or rename any layer in a click.

Precision Masking for Perfect Details

Masks give you pinpoint control over which parts of your artwork remain visible. Use a brush for freehand masking, rectangle or ellipse for neat geometric areas, or freeform to trace around complex shapes. This means you can apply an intricate scribble fill to a character’s clothing while keeping their face crisp and unfilled, or gently blend multiple fills for unique overlaps. And if you change your mind? Simply invert, expand, or refine your mask—no starting from scratch.

Layer & Mask Synergy

Combine the power of layers and masks to craft complex designs without the chaos. Place each major element—like a background, subject, or text—on its own layer, then refine individual sections using masks. For example, keep a fractal fill masked to your background layer, a wave fill masking only the subject’s silhouette, and a text fill on a separate layer for titles. This structured approach keeps every piece adjustable, so you can revisit or fine-tune details at any stage without accidentally affecting the rest of your artwork.

Vibrant Colors—Static or Dynamic

Play with color the way you’ve always wanted: pick a static hue for a consistent look, or let dynamic color sampling do the heavy lifting by pulling subtle (or dramatic) shades from your original image. This creates everything from gentle gradients to bold, colorful line work—perfect for portraits, abstract pieces, or anything in between. Want to turn up the experimental dial? Adjust segment lengths and randomization, and watch how your lines blend smoothly across the spectrum.

Add Personality with Text Fill

Pack your design with personality using Text Fill, which arranges letters or words into your artwork. Tweak spacing, font style, or word repetition to craft anything from subtle typography textures to conversation-starting statement pieces. Perfect for posters, social media graphics, or whenever you want to say something—literally—through your art.

Shape Your Artwork with Mesh

Sometimes a flat fill just isn’t enough. Enter Mesh, which bends and reshapes your fills into dynamic, custom contours. Whether you want to make a spiral wave flow with a fabric fold or wrap halftone dots around a spherical object, the Mesh tool lets you nudge nodes and handles until the lines fit every curve flawlessly.

Stroke Mastery: Dashes, Caps, and More

Not all lines are created equal! Vexy Lines offers precise stroke controls so you can tweak line thickness, add dashed patterns, and choose cap styles—round, flat, or even triangular for sharper edges. Combine these with offset parameters to create unique hatching effects, or give your illustrations an edgy, hand-inked vibe. For hair, blades of grass, or calligraphic flourishes, triangular caps with controlled transitions can really stand out.

Put It All Together—Your Style, Your Rules

From luscious scribbles to meticulously dashed outlines, every advanced fill setting in Vexy Lines works together seamlessly. Layer them up, blend them, or mask them away—whatever suits your creative flow. The freedom to mix dynamic color, text, mesh transformations, and refined strokes means there’s no limit to the art you can produce.
